Louis Vuitton is a French luxury fashion brand founded in 1854 by Louis Vuitton.
It is renowned for its high-end leather goods, handbags, clothing, shoes, accessories, and fragrances. 
The brand's signature monogram canvas and craftsmanship make it a symbol of luxury and elegance. Louis
Vuitton is part of LVMH, the world's largest luxury goods company.  
    The brand is known for its innovation, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern designs. It 
frequently collaborates with artists and designers, creating exclusive collections. Louis Vuitton 
products are handcrafted with premium materials, ensuring durability and sophistication. The company 
operates flagship stores in major cities worldwide, attracting fashion enthusiasts and celebrities. 
Over the years, it has maintained its reputation as one of the most valuable luxury brands globally.
